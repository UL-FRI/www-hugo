Assist. David Jelenc, PhD
Assistant
T:
E: david.jelenc@fri.uni-lj.si
Office hours:
Room: R3.50 - Laboratorij LEM

David Jelenc obtained his BSc and his PhD at University of Ljubljana (Faculty of Computer and Information Science). He is with the Laboratory for e-media where he works as a researcher and teaching assistant. His research interests include computational trust and reputation models, agent-based simulations, computer networks and information security.
