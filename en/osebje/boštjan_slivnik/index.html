Assist. Prof. Boštjan Slivnik, PhD
Assistant Professor
T:
E: bostjan.slivnik@fri.uni-lj.si
Office hours:
Room: R2.14 - Kabinet

Boštjan Slivnik is an Assistant Professor. His main areas of research include compilers and grid techology. Activities and Events

Program Committee member of The Technical Track on “Programming Languages” of The ACM Symposium on Applied Computing (Fortaleza, Ceara, Brazil, March 16-20, 2008). Program Committee member 1st Workshop on Advances in Programming Languages (WAPL 2007) of International Multiconference on Computer Science and Information Technology (Wisla, Poland, October 15-17, 2007). Program Committee member of The Technical Track on “Programming Languages” of The ACM Symposium on Applied Computing (Seoul, Korea, March 11-15, 2007).
